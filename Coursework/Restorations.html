Oil Paintings

We offer a broad range of oil painting based restoration services with a world-renowned attention to every brush stroke, aiming fix and enhance any damaged areas whilst preserving the original nature of the piece. Whether there are cuts, liquid spills or signs of colour fading; one of our highly experienced restoration experts will restore your piece to its former glory.

Reframing

A wide range of frame materials, sizes and types are available for purchase. Whether you are looking for minimalist and contemporary or grand and extravagant, we are sure to find the right fit. Included in the price is an expert removal and re-fitting service where a trained technician will remove the old frame and reframe it with your newly purchased one.

Fire Damage

If your artworks suffer substantial fire damage, we're here to help. The most important thing to remember is not to move or interact with the damaged piece; leave that to our trained restoration technicians. We will also require high-quality images or scans of the artwork, before it was damaged, to aid the re-creation process. As this is a highly specialized service we offer, price will fluctuate based on the value of the artwork and the extent of the damage.
