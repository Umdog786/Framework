Our Story

Framework has been specialising in landscape and old master's paintings for over 10 years. Our collection is one of the largest in the country and showcases 500 years of British & European art from the 1600s through to the late 20th century. We focus our current collection on the renaissance and romanticism art movements, ranging from striking seascapes to bustling Venetian landscapes.
